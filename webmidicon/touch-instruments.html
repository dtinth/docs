Play notes with your touchscreen

WebMIDICon comes with multiple touch instruments that can be played with your touchscreen.

Keyboards

Piano

A normal piano keyboard. It has 3 rows, giving you access to 4 octaves.

Jammer

An isomorphic keyboard based on the Jammer (Wicki-Hayden) note layout.

Sonome

An isomorphic keyboard based on the Sonome (harmonic table) note layout.

C-System

An isomorphic keyboard based on the C-system layout, as used in Chromatic button accordions.

Drums

Drum pad

A 4x4 drum pad that lets you finger drum. It supports variable velocity, producing natural-sounding drum hits.

Pedals

iPedal

Put your device on the floor and use it as a sustain pedal or a kick drum.
